WalkMe
walkme is a safety-enhanced pedestrian routing application. it recommends the safest path from A to B based on recorded coordinate-specific crime data from NYC city data. walkme offers a unique solution to situations when you might be walking alone in an unfamiliar city and would rather optimize for safety rather than distance. major features include displaying route options ranked by a safety index from 1 to 10, which is calculated using a custom heuristic, as well as infographic popups for each route with more statistics that give context for what a certain safety index really means.

we used the Ionic framework, built on top of Angular JS, for multi-platform support. we used a Django backend with a PostgreSQL database. my focus was on the backend and heuristic design.
